Gardening Tips & Useful Links

Helpful gardening tips and recommended resources to enhance your organic gardening experience.

Top Gardening Tips

  • Watering Properly: Water your garden early in the morning or late in the evening to prevent moisture loss through evaporation.
  • Healthy Soil: Add compost, mulch, and other organic matter to keep your soil rich in nutrients.
  • Natural Pest Control: Use companion planting, neem oil, and beneficial insects like ladybugs to manage pests without chemicals.
  • Crop Rotation: Rotate your crops every season to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest infestations.
  • Pruning and Maintenance: Regularly prune your plants to remove dead or diseased growth and encourage healthy new growth.
